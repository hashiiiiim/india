Why India?

India Tourism Highlights

  • Home to 38 UNESCO World Heritage Sites
  • Diverse landscapes from Himalayas to beaches
  • Rich cultural heritage spanning 5000+ years
  • World's largest democracy with 1.4 billion people
  • 200+ recognized languages and dialects

2025 Travel Trends

  • Wellness tourism with Ayurveda and yoga retreats
  • Luxury train journeys like Palace on Wheels
  • Adventure tourism in the Himalayas
  • Cultural immersion with rural tourism
  • Wildlife safaris in national parks

Best Time to Visit

  • October-March: Ideal for most regions
  • April-June: Best for hill stations
  • July-September: Monsoon magic in Kerala
  • November-February: Perfect for Rajasthan
  • Year-round: Goa, Kerala backwaters
